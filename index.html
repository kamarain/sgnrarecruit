Call for Research Assistants

We are seeking research assistants to our research teams in Signal Processing Research Centre at Tampere University.

The positions will offer excellent opportunities to work in a team of professionals responsible for developing cutting edge technologies, and allows you to learn many aspects from fundamental research problems to concrete applications.

Applicant

We welcome applications with any research focus related to signal processing, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and robotics. Ideal candidate has a strong programming and mathematics background. Experience in (or strong will to learn) programming with Python or C++/Java are considered as advantages. The candidates is studying in relevant BSc or MSc programs of Tampere University.

Team and research

The doctoral candidates will be supervised by PIs from the research groups of TAU Signal Processing Research Center (SGN). We work broadly in the fields of imaging, computer vision, audio processing, signal processing, machine learning, and robotics. More information of our research is available on the research centre web pages linked below.
